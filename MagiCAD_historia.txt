Founded in 1983 as Progman in Rauma, Finland, MagiCAD Group has over 40 years of experience in MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) design software. The company revolutionized HVAC design in the 1990s by introducing intelligent, automated 3D modeling for AutoCAD, with Revit support following, and became part of Glodon Company Limited in 2014.

Key highlights of MagiCAD's history include:
Origins (1980s): Founded as Progman, initially focusing on customized software before moving toward specialized MEP products.
The "Magic" of Automation (1990s): The software was named "MagiCAD" because it could automatically handle complex tasks—such as turning bends into T-branches instantly—which previously required hours of manual drafting.
Platform Expansion: The company developed MagiCAD for AutoCAD and later MagiCAD for Revit, becoming a leading BIM solution in Europe.
Growth and Internationalization: In 2014, Progman was acquired by Glodon Company Limited. The company has continued to expand its global presence, including founding a dedicated German subsidiary in 2022.
Modern Focus (2020s): Today, MagiCAD Group focuses on, BIM, sustainability in building services, and providing a comprehensive database of real, manufacturer-specific products for design calculation.
